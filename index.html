What this repository is — and isn't

AI — systems that produce content, predictions, recommendations, or decisions to achieve specific goals — is now woven through the behavioural research workflow. Used well, it can enhance what we do. Used badly, it can mislead research and harm participants.

This repository is a living, curated guide built by the Behavioural Research UK community. It is not definitive, and we are not responsible for the external tools and resources signposted within it — see the full disclaimer. Keep in mind the maxim: poor-quality input leads to poor-quality output.
