Section 3

General AI Learning

A curated set of learning resources, including webinars, courses, and videos — from beginner-friendly primers to advanced deep-learning programmes. Use these to build intuition about how AI works before applying it in your own research.

On this page

  • BR-UK AI Webinars
  • Online courses
  • YouTube channels
  • Advanced learning

BR-UK AI Webinars

BR-UK has conducted three webinars on AI in behavioural research — available on demand via our webinars & events page:

  • Using Artificial Intelligence to improve Behavioural Research
  • Using Analytic AI to improve Behavioural Research
  • Responsible use of AI in behavioural research

Online courses

Elements of AI

Offered by the University of Helsinki and MinnaLearn, this course aims to demystify AI through theoretical and practical exercises.

AI for Beginners

Developed by Microsoft, this 12-week, 24-lesson curriculum provides an introduction to AI.

Google Cloud ML & AI Courses

Google offers a range of courses covering machine learning and AI fundamentals for every skill level.

Khan Academy — AI for Education

Offers a 3-unit course on generative AI addressing topics like what it is and best practices for teaching and learning.

IBM SkillsBuild — AI

IBM SkillsBuild offers a series of online lectures introducing artificial intelligence, generative AI, and AI ethics.

YouTube channels

Andy Stapleton

A YouTube channel designed to assist academics, particularly PhD students and early-career researchers, in navigating the complexities of academia and integrating AI into their workflows.

WiseUp Communications

Offers practical guidance on research writing, presentations, and some videos on leveraging AI tools for academic purposes.

Prof. David Stuckler

Discusses the use of AI in academic research processes, focusing on maintaining academic integrity while leveraging AI.

Dr. Philip Adu

Focuses on using AI tools to empower qualitative and quantitative research methods.

Advanced learning resources

Harvard CS50's Introduction to AI with Python

Self-paced 7-week online course on edX offered by Harvard University, covering graph-search algorithms, reinforcement learning, AI principles, how to design intelligent systems, and how to use AI in Python programmes.

Practical Deep Learning for Coders

Fast.ai offers a course designed to make deep learning accessible for those with some coding experience.

Cooperative AI Interactive Course

This course from BlueDot Impact introduces an emerging research field focused on improving AI's cooperative intelligence for the benefit of all.
